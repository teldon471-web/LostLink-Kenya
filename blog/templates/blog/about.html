‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is LostLink Kenya free?

Yes! LostLink Kenya is completely free to use. We believe in community support without barriers.

How long do posts stay active?

Posts remain active for 90 days by default. You can mark items as "Resolved" when recovered.

Can I contact other users?

Yes! You can message other users directly through the platform to discuss item recovery.

How do I prove ownership?

When contacted about a match, you can describe unique identifying features to verify ownership.

Is my personal information safe?

Your information is protected. You can control how much information you share with other users.

What if I find an item?

Post it on LostLink Kenya and wait for the owner to contact you. Include as many details as possible!
